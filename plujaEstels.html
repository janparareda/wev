What format is the most optimal for this type of image and why? With what compression quality does the image not lose quality? Can we reduce its size even more without losing quality? What would happen if we save the image several times?
The best format for this type of image is PNG because it does not lose quality when you save it. The image does not lose quality until you save it at 60% compression quality. If you save the image several times, it will lose quality.
